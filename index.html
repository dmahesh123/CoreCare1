"Supporting Mental Health in Construction"

Procare in partnership with B1M is committed to enhancing the mental health of construction workers who face on-site risks. We prioritize mental health resources to create a safer, more supportive work environment. Our chatbot, developed with Procare, offers guidance and support for improved well-being. Together, we aim to build a safer and more resilient workforce.

Favorite Pastimes

Inclusion

Uniting inclusion and mental health is vital for construction workers, fostering a diverse, stigma-free workplace that improves overall well-being and work performance.

Lead

Balancing the responsibilities of leadership in construction with prioritizing mental health is pivotal for creating a supportive work environment that fosters both productivity and well-being among team members.

Safety and Health

Ensuring safety and health in construction is crucial for safeguarding the lives and livelihoods of construction workers, enabling them to work confidently and securely.
